What is AU Lotto and how does it work?

AU Lotto is a lottery game where players select a set of numbers to match the drawn results. You win prizes based on how many numbers you match. The more numbers you match, the bigger the prize.

How can I purchase AU Lotto tickets?

You can buy AU Lotto tickets online or at authorized retailers. Simply choose your numbers or use the quick pick option, then confirm your ticket before the draw closes.

When are AU Lotto draws held?

AU Lotto draws typically take place on specific days each week. Check the official schedule on our platform to ensure you don’t miss out on participating in upcoming draws.

How do I claim my AU Lotto winnings?
