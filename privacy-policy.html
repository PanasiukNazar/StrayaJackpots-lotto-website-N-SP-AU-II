Cookie Policy

Introduction

This Cookie Policy outlines how cookies and similar technologies are used on the AU Lotto platform to enhance your experience, collect non-personalized data, and provide relevant services. By using the AU Lotto website, you consent to the use of cookies as described below.

Types of Cookies Used on AU Lotto

1. Essential Cookies

These cookies are required to ensure the basic functionality and security of the AU Lotto platform. They enable key features like login authentication, site navigation, and system integrity. Disabling these cookies may affect your ability to use the platform efficiently.

2. Performance Cookies

Performance cookies collect anonymous data to help us understand how users interact with the AU Lotto site. Information such as page visits, time on site, and technical errors allow us to continuously improve the quality and usability of the platform.

3. Functionality Cookies

These cookies store preferences such as remembered login details or selected settings on AU Lotto. They help deliver a more customized and user-friendly experience by adapting to your personal preferences.

4. Marketing and Analytics Cookies

AU Lotto may use marketing and analytics cookies to understand user interaction with promotional content and services. These cookies support delivering more relevant messaging and help optimize advertising campaigns within the AU Lotto platform.

Purpose of Cookie Usage

Cookies help AU Lotto provide a seamless experience by:

  • Keeping you signed in during your session.
  • Tailoring the interface and recommendations to your interests.
  • Analyzing performance and resolving technical issues.
  • Supporting delivery of targeted and relevant content.

Managing Your Cookies

You can manage or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Be aware that some cookies are essential for proper operation of the AU Lotto website, and disabling them may reduce functionality or prevent access to certain features.

Use of Third-Party Cookies

We may collaborate with trusted third-party services that use cookies to support analytics or deliver content related to AU Lotto. These parties only receive limited data required to fulfill their roles, and personal data is not shared unnecessarily.

Data Protection and Security

AU Lotto takes appropriate technical measures to protect information collected through cookies. While we maintain strict safeguards, please note that no digital system is completely immune to potential risks.

Updates to This Policy

We may revise this Cookie Policy to align with changes in technology or regulations. Updated versions will be published on this page. Continued use of the AU Lotto platform indicates your acceptance of the latest policy terms.
