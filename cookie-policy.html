Cookie Policy

Introduction

This Cookie Policy outlines how cookies and similar tracking technologies are used on the AU Lotto website to improve user experience and deliver customized lottery-related content.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you access the AU Lotto site. They enable us to recognize your browser, remember your preferences, and enhance your interaction with our platform.

Types of Cookies Used

1. Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are required for the AU Lotto site to function correctly. They support secure login, page navigation, and essential user session management during your interaction with AU Lotto services.

2. Performance Cookies

These cookies collect anonymous statistical data to help us analyze how users engage with AU Lotto pages. They allow us to identify which areas are performing well and where improvements are needed.

3. Functional Cookies

Functional cookies help us remember your personal settings on the AU Lotto platform, such as your preferred lottery type or display options, to enhance convenience and provide a more personalized experience.

4. Targeting Cookies

Targeting cookies are used to deliver lottery offers, promotions, and jackpot updates tailored to your preferences. These cookies help ensure you receive the most relevant information while using AU Lotto.

How AU Lotto Uses Cookies

AU Lotto uses cookies to:

  • Remember your login details and account settings.
  • Provide personalized lottery recommendations and jackpot alerts.
  • Track usage patterns to improve AU Lotto site features.
  • Deliver promotional messages related to AU Lotto draws.

Managing Cookies

You can configure your browser settings to manage or delete cookies at any time. Please note that disabling essential cookies may impact your ability to fully use the AU Lotto website or access certain features.

Your Consent

By continuing to use the AU Lotto website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy. You may withdraw your consent by adjusting your cookie settings in your browser.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on AU Lotto may be placed by authorized third-party services that support analytics, content personalization, or campaign performance tracking. These partners follow their own cookie policies and handle data in accordance with privacy standards.

Updates to This Cookie Policy

AU Lotto may revise this Cookie Policy from time to time in response to technological or regulatory developments. All updates will be posted on this page. We recommend reviewing it periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding the use of cookies on the AU Lotto platform, please contact us. Our team is committed to ensuring your privacy is respected and protected at all times.
